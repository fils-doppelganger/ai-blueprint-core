# GLOSSARY.md Format

Canonical terminology for the teaching workspace.

## Rules
- Add terms only after the learner can *use* them correctly in context.
- Tight 1–2 sentence "IS" definitions (not "how to").
- Be opinionated on aliases and preferred terms.
- Use glossary terms inside other definitions.
- Group under natural subheadings when clusters emerge.
- Revise in place as understanding deepens.
- Flag field ambiguities explicitly.
